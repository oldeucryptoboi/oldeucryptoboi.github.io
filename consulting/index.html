Consulting

Enterprise AI agent governance, safety, and architecture

Deploying AI agents in production requires more than prompt engineering. It requires governance frameworks, safety guarantees, and deterministic execution. I help teams build agent systems that are accountable, auditable, and safe to operate at scale.

What I Offer

Agent Governance Design

Design delegation frameworks, authority models, and accountability primitives for your AI agent systems. Based on Google DeepMind's Intelligent AI Delegation research.

Safety & Compliance Architecture

Circuit breakers, futility monitoring, cost controls, and audit trails. Build agent systems that satisfy compliance requirements without sacrificing autonomy.

Deterministic Runtime Implementation

Hash-chain journals, reproducible execution, and tamper-evident logging. Make your agent systems debuggable, auditable, and provable.

Multi-Agent System Design

P2P delegation meshes, trust propagation, and inter-agent coordination. Architecture for systems where agents delegate to other agents.
